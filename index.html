Sunny Street

Participated in VizForSocialGood project for September month where we tried to explore the amazing work that Sunny Street as an organization is doing. They are a nonprofit organization operating in Australia that organizes health camps with the help of Volunteers, Sponsors for the underprivileged ones (homeless, refuge, not able to afford ) at free of cost services. The aim of the Viz is to create awareness about the works of Sunny Street and ask volunteers and donators to show love and support.
